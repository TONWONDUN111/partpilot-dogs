Contact us at: contact@partpilotmarket.com

PartPilot Market participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means we may earn a small commission when you click a link on our site and make a purchase, at no additional cost to you. We are also a member of the eBay Partner Network and other affiliate programs.

Our editorial content is not influenced by affiliate relationships. We link to products we believe are genuinely helpful to automotive shoppers.

3. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Repair help requests: If you submit a repair help form, we collect the name, contact information, vehicle details, and repair description you provide. This information is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) by default and is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly contact us.

Information collected automatically

  • Browser storage: We use localStorage to save your filter preferences and any repair help requests you submit. This data exists only on your device and is never sent to us.
  • No tracking cookies: We do not set first-party tracking cookies or collect IP addresses. Third-party links (Amazon, eBay, etc.) are subject to their own privacy policies.

4. How we use your information

We use the information you provide only to:

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.
